The number of conferences and workshops on topics related to agent-based computational economics (ACE) is now so large that up-to-date information can no longer be provided at the ACE website. Interested readers might wish to subscribe to the Society for Computational Economics (SCE) mailing list , and/or to the Simulation of Society (SimSoc) mailing list which focuses more generally on computer simulation in the social sciences. Various announcements about upcoming conference and workshops are frequently posted to these lists.
